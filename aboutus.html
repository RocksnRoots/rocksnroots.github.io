About us

Jyotsna and Ravi Dayal bring the 5 elements of nature together to create beautiful green spaces. They are qualified Urban planners and Architects respectively. Their journey started from bare building blocks and barren land to
green mounds and forests. They steer a technical organization that goes into the R&D of every design and concept that they have to execute. Their forte is in plant selection(nationally and internationally), plant layouts, color pallets in greenery and container selection
